# beicraft
Agro Management System (AMS) is a platform hosted by CHREAM homes Limited and designed to assist businessmen venture into agriculture with little or less knowledge in the agro sector, whilst it uses its tools to aid effective management and growth of the venture with little effort. All research done here was harnessed to help provide a solution to some of the problems faced in the farming sector, bridge the farmer-marketing gaps. This app creates an initiative to promote the use of insurance in SMEs in Nigeria and Africa at large and also assurance of security from registered agencies it seeks  to support its course  through creating sanctions where applicable.
